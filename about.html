Real-World Applications

The Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP) is a fundamental optimization challenge with numerous real-world applications:

  • Logistics and Delivery Route Optimization
  • Circuit Board Drilling and Manufacturing
  • DNA Sequencing in Bioinformatics
  • Network Design and Data Routing
  • Tour Planning and Travel Itineraries

Our Genetic Algorithm Solution

We've implemented a sophisticated genetic algorithm that mimics natural evolution to find optimal solutions:

1. Population Initialization

Creates multiple potential routes between cities

2. Fitness Evaluation

Calculates total distance for each route

3. Selection

Chooses the best routes for reproduction

4. Crossover

Combines routes to create new solutions

5. Mutation

Introduces random changes to maintain diversity
